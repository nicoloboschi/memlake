## Notes

- `exact` is the reference ceiling: brute-force numpy cosine over the cached
  vectors plus a dependency-free Okapi BM25. It validates the harness itself.
- `exact` sparse is a light-stemming BM25 and scores slightly below Anserini;
  `qdrant` sparse uses fastembed BM25 tokenization with server-side IDF.
- Latencies are single-query, sequential, warm, measured client-side. Qdrant
  numbers therefore include HTTP round-trip and are not directly comparable to
  the in-process `exact` numbers.
